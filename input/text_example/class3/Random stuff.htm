Filed under News and useless stuff.

Who is Robin Kraft?

Robin Kraft is a 26 years old mother of 5 children. She has been found guilty of abuse on one of her 5 children in Hamilton County, Ohio. Now, it is not an uncommon thing for a man to get caught doing that, but for a mother, it’s exceptional.

I know that the “mother instinct” thing is not real (facts proves that the mother instinct appeared around the same time as the medecine evolved to a point that mortality rate dropped) but still, how could a mother do that? Let’s leave the mother instinct aside for a moment and let us go deeper into human psyche. Pure survival instinct.

The survival of the species is one of the most powerful instinct that nature gave us and a person like Robin Kraft is just destroying the will of her kids. What will happen to those kids? I don’t know. I don’t know how social services are in USA but I know that here, in Canada, they sucks. Badly. So now, the kids will be marked for life by that undeserving mother and maybe by the social services. Good future, hey?

By the way, I liked the reason the judge gave for the 40 years of prison the mother got, I quote: “Hamilton County Common Pleas Judge David Davis on Friday imposed the maximum sentence, saying Kraft should not be released from prison while she can bear children.“.

I wonder if prisons for women have the same problems as the men prisons, namely drugs and “punish the child molestors”.

Anyway, it’s a sad story.
